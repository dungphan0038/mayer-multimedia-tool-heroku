MAYER'S MULTIMEDIA DESIGN TOOL

I. Provide Your Project Details

Select the type(s) of your multimedia project. This helps identify the format of your content, such as an online course, video, or app. Choose "Other" to specify a custom type if needed.

Choose the audience(s) for your project. This defines who will use or view your content, such as students or professionals. Select "Other" to specify a unique audience.

Select the goal(s) of your project. This indicates the purpose, such as informing, educating, or engaging users. Choose "Other" to specify a custom goal.

Choose the prior knowledge level(s) of your audience. This indicates their familiarity with the topic, from beginner to expert, to tailor the content appropriately.

Select the media type(s) used in your project, such as text, images, or video. This defines the content elements. Choose "Other" to specify a unique medium.

Select the duration(s) of your content, such as short or long segments. This helps structure the content. Choose "Other" to specify a custom duration.

Choose the interaction level(s) of your project, such as passive viewing or active quizzes. This defines user engagement. Select "Other" for a custom interaction.

Describe your project in detail (max 500 characters). Include specific goals, content, or context to help generate accurate design recommendations.
